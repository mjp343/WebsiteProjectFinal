Flow Jiu Jitsu

Martial Arts Academy in Cartagena, Colombia

Flow Jiu Jitsu

Flow Jiu Jitsu, our academy, is located in the beautiful city of Cartagena, Colombia. We're lucky to have a stunning beach view right outside our doors. At Flow Jiu Jitsu, we offer top-notch martial arts training within a friendly and welcoming community. Whether you're a local or just visiting, you'll find a place here to learn, grow, and share your passion for Jiu Jitsu. Our academy is more than just a training space; it's a spot where people come together, enjoy the endless summer vibes, and make lasting friendships, all under the backdrop of Cartagena's beautiful beaches.

Where the Waves Meet the Mats

Classes for Everybody!

At Flow Jiu Jitsu, we offer a wide range of classes to cater to all interests and skill levels. Whether you're interested in self-defense, want to master the art of gi grappling, or prefer the dynamic nature of no-gi grappling, we have a class for you. Our curriculum also includes judo and wrestling, providing a comprehensive grappling experience that emphasizes technique, balance, and power. For those looking to round out their martial arts training, our striking classes focus on boxing and Muay Thai fundamentals, teaching precision, agility, and strength. Each class is designed to foster growth, resilience, and a deep understanding of martial arts, ensuring that every member of our community can find their path in the martial arts journey.
